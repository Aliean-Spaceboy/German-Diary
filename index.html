    📖 Study Guide & Wiki

    Follow this daily routine for the fastest results to B2 fluency.

    ⏱️ The Optimal Daily Routine (20-30 Minutes)

    1. Check Dashboard (2m): Read Word/Sentence of the Day. Listen & repeat.
    2. Review SRS (5m): Open More > SRS Review. Add 5-10 new words you encountered.
    3. Write Diary (10m): Open Diary tab. Use Sentence Builder first. Write freely.
    4. Take a Quiz (5m): Alternate daily: MCQ, Listening, Grammar, Verbs.
    💡 Weekly Habit: Every Sunday, open the Weekly Reflection tab to review your progress!

    📈 Adapt for Each Level

    🟢 A1 (Beginner)

    Add everyday words. Write 1 simple sentence per prompt (e.g., Ich lerne Deutsch).

    🟡 A2 (Elementary)

    Write 2 sentences per prompt. Combine them using und, aber, weil. Train your ear with Dictation.

    🟠 B1 (Intermediate)

    Import IT Job Dataset. Write in past tense (Perfekt). Record a monthly 1-minute speech.

    🔴 B2 (Fluent)

    Read DW news daily. Master irregular verbs. Write full paragraphs using the strict Grammar Checklist.
